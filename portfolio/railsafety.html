Freight Safety in the US

I designed this story map after conducting a series of interviews with freight train conductors about rail safety. The interviews were for a UW-Madison course project in the spring of 2024, but I wanted to highlight them in a visual format. What the conductors shared with me shifted my understanding of the research on rail safety that I had read prior to speaking with them. I got a better understanding of how deeply related public safety and freight working conditions were. I wanted to highlight that relationship in this story map assignment for Geography 572: Graphic Design in Cartography. This Esri story map takes the reader through key risk factors and consequences of train derailments in the US. It incorporates several maps that I created using ArcGIS Pro. I chose to use dark colors with red and orange accents to evoke a somber mood that fit the topic.
